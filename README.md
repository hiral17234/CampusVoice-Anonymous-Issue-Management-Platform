# 🎓 CampusVoice  
### A Community-Driven Campus Issue Reporting Platform

> **When voices are scattered, we help them get found.**

🔗 **Live Demo:** (https://campusvoice-chi.vercel.app/) 
📌 **Status:** Active Development

---

## 🌟 What is CampusVoice?

**CampusVoice** is a centralized platform that empowers students to **report, track, and amplify campus issues** in a transparent and structured way.

It creates a direct, accountable bridge between **students and campus administration**, ensuring real problems get real attention.

## 🧠 Why CampusVoice?

Traditional campus communication systems are often:
- Fragmented
- Non-transparent
- Slow
- Hard to track

**CampusVoice solves this by providing:**
- A single trusted platform
- Visibility through community support
- Accountability through status tracking
- A voice that actually leads to action
